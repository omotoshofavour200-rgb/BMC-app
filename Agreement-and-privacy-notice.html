Independent Contractor Agreement & Privacy Notice

All partners and independent contractors of BLC Solutions, LLC are required to complete and sign a W2 agreement and submit a valid IRS W2 form before receiving any payments.

While IRS W2 does not expire, it must be updated if your legal name, address, or taxpayer identification number changes. BLC Solutions, LLC may request updated W2 forms periodically to ensure records remain accurate and compliant.
